呼吸 · Two Breaths

Two ways of loving, side by side

One guards by pushing everyone away. One guards by pulling everyone close. Neither is wrong — they simply breathe in different directions.

風
Wind Hashira spirit
Sanemi
Sharp-tongued. Fiercely protective. Soft only where no one is looking.

He loves the way wind moves through leaves — unseen, but undeniable. He'll never say the gentle thing out loud, so he shows it instead: standing between you and the cold, staying close without ever admitting why.

"I don't need to be liked. I need you to be standing tomorrow."

蝶
Flower Hashira spirit
Kanae
Warm-eyed. Endlessly patient. Kind even to those who don't deserve it yet.

She loves the way wisteria loves a trellis — quietly, completely, climbing without needing to be asked. She smiles first, listens longer, and believes people can soften even when they insist they can't.

"You don't have to be gentle for me to love you gently."

Where the wind
learns to bloom

A wisteria vine can survive the wildest wind if it simply learns to bend instead of break. And a wind that loves a flower learns, slowly, to blow a little softer — just for her. This is that in-between place: not calm, not chaos, but two forces who decided the other was worth changing shape for.

Akshat ♡ Amisha

Like the wind that never stops moving toward what it protects, and the wisteria that never stops climbing toward the light — Akshat promises Amisha forever: through every storm, to be the calm she can lean on, and through every season, to be the one who chooses her again, quietly, completely, always.

Not just today. Not just for now. Forever.
